ABOUT EDGLOBE

Mathematics and Science being compulsory subject of study, access to quality education is every child's right. Humans cannot remember the words spoken unless it is translated into picture slides. Normally every word spoken is being translated by us into motion pictures by visualizing the words and then by labeling it. To a certain extent, laboratories for core subjects and the pictures shown in the text books helps the students to visualize most of the things learned. But it has its own limitation. On the other hand, core subject education especially mathematics in our schools is beset with problems. Due to the hierarchy of concepts and largely deductive and abstract nature of the subject, mathematics is considered as a very dull and difficult subject. It limits the scope of visualization due to the complexity nature of the subject.

A shift in focus is needed in core subject teaching and learning. HRD departments and educational institutions of developed countries addressed the above problem and recommended the use of smart class for teaching complicated subjects. The National Curriculum Framework for School education by various authorities and boards in India also strongly suggest the use of smart class for visualization of concepts.

To contribute for the quality of education in India and to address above problem IERT GLOBAL PVT LTD, has taken initiative in the marketing and promotion of a syllabus based educational software that enable students to set up a smart class at their home at an affordable cost. The smart class promoted by IERT GLOBAL branded as “EDGLOBE” Series, helps the student to learn most complicated subjects and topics in simple language providing visual aids and graphics which will help the students to remember things learned. Most of the topics and lessons are explained using visual display of images, graphics, motion pictures and animations with voice over.
